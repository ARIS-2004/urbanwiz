# Design System

## Don'ts
- No stock illustrations
- No AI-generated people
- No giant icons
- Avoid gradients

## Rules
- Rounded corners: 28px
- Card shadows: very soft
- Use large typography
- Section spacing: 160px
- Max width: 1440px
- Content width: 1200px
- Glassmorphism: ~10% opacity only
- Navigation: transparent and sticky
- Buttons: height 56px
- Hero headings: 72px desktop
- Subheading: 20px

## Tone
Editorial. Apple, Stripe, Linear. Handcrafted and premium.

## Colors
- Deep Navy: #0F1330
- Primary Blue: #1E3ABA
- Orange Accent: #FF5722
- White: #FFFFFF
- Background: #FAFAFA

## Fonts
- Main: Satoshi Variable
- Headings: General Sans Variable
- Luxury accent: Instrument Serif (italic)
